Here is the core idea.

You visit a restaurant, café or bar
and order online.

Trust Problems

  • How can the location representative confirm, that an order was placed by the one sitting at the table?
  • How can a user confirm, that he orders at the right location?
  • How can the location representative confirm, that the user paid?

Solution

  • The location representative generates a code, which can be scanned by the user.
  • By scanning, the user obtains a key and by that access to the order management.
  • Payments will not be supported at first and must be done be the existing payment system.
